Site Name

Rain/Shine Weather Service

Purpose

To provide accurate weather forecasting with a specialization on what to expect as an outdoor laborer/worker. Most weather providers just tell the numbers, but we want to help users understand how those numbers will affect their working experience so they can look forward to the day or brace for it.
